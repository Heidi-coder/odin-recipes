Whatever-You-Want Salad

Description

First up on our list of ADHD-friendly recipes, we've got Whatever-you-want salad! While this may sound like a cop-out for recipe-writing, I promise this will solve a lot of your meal worries. This is what saved me when I was sick of eating processed pre-made meals and wanted something healthier.

The basic gist of this recipe is that you can go to the store and buy cans of food that look like they'll go well together and the occasional pre-washed fresh vegetable, if you so desire, and dump them in one large leftover container. Don't dump them in a bowl! It's worth it to invest in a large leftover container to avoid any more dirty dishes than are necessary.

The following recipe is one version of whatever-you-want salad that I like and would recommend.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans black beans
  • 2 cans corn
  • 1 can Hunts diced tomatoes
  • 3 stalks of pre-washed, pre-cut celery, optional
  • Squeeze sour cream

Steps

  1. Open and drain any cans of food.
  2. Dump all the canned food into the leftover container.
  3. Break, or cut if you feel like it, the celery stalks into the container.
  4. Stir it all up.
  5. Squeeze the desired amount of sour cream on top.
  6. Pro tip: Leave a spoon in the container in the fridge so you won't have to wash one every time you want to eat some. Unless, of course, it's a family dish and you don't all want to use the same spoon!
