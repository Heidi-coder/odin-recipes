Microwaved Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

This recipe is a little more straitforward. Basically, it's a grilled-cheese sandwich but done in the microwave. The only catch is that you need a toaster.

You're probably wondering whether it's actually any good, though. It may not taste exactly like your perfect idea of a grilled cheese sandwich, but it is a delicious meal that you will definitely still enjoy if you are a fan of grilled cheese.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread.
  • Margarine or butter, whichever you prefer
  • The desired amount of cheese, preferrably pre-sliced

Steps

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of toast.
  3. Put the cheese on the buttered side and put the other slice on top with the buttered side touching the cheese.
  4. Put the sandwich on a microwave-safe plate and microwave it for thirty seconds.
  5. Enjoy!
